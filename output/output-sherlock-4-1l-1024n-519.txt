"this is too bad of you, mcmurdo!" he said. "if i guarantee them, that is enough for you. there is the young lady, too. she cannot wait on the public road at this hour." "very sorry, mr. thaddeus," said the porter, inexorably. "folk may be friends o' yours, and yet no friends o' the master's. he pays me well to do my duty, and my duty i'll do. i don't know none o' your friends." "oh, yes you do, mcmurdo," cried sherlock holmes, genially.
